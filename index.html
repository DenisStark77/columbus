Project Challenges

Columbus Heights Estate is a unique development project. Here are some challenges that potential buyers should be aware of:

No Electricity

The property currently has no connection to the electrical grid. Owners will need to implement off-grid solutions such as solar panels.

Solution: Solar power systems can be installed at an additional cost.

Community Conflicts

There have been some historical disputes between community members regarding property boundaries and use of common areas.

Solution: We're establishing a formal HOA with clear bylaws to prevent future conflicts.

Water Leakages

During heavy rainy seasons, some lots may experience water runoff issues due to the mountainous terrain of the property.

Solution: Proper drainage systems should be implemented during construction.

Road Access

Access roads to some lots may require additional maintenance, especially during the rainy season when erosion can occur.

Solution: Regular road maintenance is planned as part of the community association.

Internet Connectivity

Due to the remote location, high-speed internet options are limited. Satellite internet is currently the most viable option.

Solution: We're in talks with several providers to bring better connectivity to the area.

Construction Logistics

Building materials need to be transported up the mountain, which can increase construction costs and timeline.

Solution: We can recommend contractors who specialize in building in remote locations.
