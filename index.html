Location & context

Columbus Heights Estate is located in the hills above Costa Rica’s central Pacific coast.

  • 8 minutes drive to Playa Hermosa, a world-class surf beach
  • 12 minutes drive to Esterillos
  • No resort zone, no vacation rental strip

This area attracts people who enjoy the ocean but prefer to live outside tourist-driven environments.

What this is
  • Independently owned fincas

Decentralization as a feature

There is no central authority enforcing rules, timelines, or standards.

This allows freedom and low ongoing costs, but requires personal responsibility. People who expect systems to take care of them usually struggle here.
